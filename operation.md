## The PSWS Control System

The PSWS nodal network is managed by **Bill Engelke AB4EJ**, with servers and support from the University of Alabama. Bill is retired from the university.

If you are looking to build a PSWS, you will need to join the **HamSCI PSWS Google Groups** and request Bill's email address. Creation of node IDs is a manual process.
