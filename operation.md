# Operation and HamSCI PSWS Community  (WIP)
## The PSWS Control System -  
The PSWS nodal-network is managed by Bill ENGELKE AB4EJ with servers and support from the University of Alabama. Bill is retired from the university. If you are looking to build a PSWS, you will need to become of member of the HamSCI PSWS Google Groups and request Bill's email address. Creation of node ID's is a manual process.
